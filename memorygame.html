HOW TO PLAY

When you click the "START GAME" button, you will have 50 seconds to find all the matching picture cards. Click on a picture card to turn it over and reveal an image of something that pollutes our oceans.

If you can find its pair, you have found a match! When you find a match, both cards will stay faced up and your 'matched cards' score will go up by 1. If you have not found two identical cards, they will automatically turn back around.

To win the game you must find all the matches before the time runs out. The ocean and its wildlife are counting on you!
